inlinestatic

Performs clipping on the two polygons clipping_points and subjected_points (the technique used i Sutherland-Hodgman clipping) and returns the overlapping polygon result_points. The method works in 3D. Both polygons have to be convex, but they can be slightly perturbated in 3D space, this allows for performing clipping on two interpolated interfaces

Parameters
clipping_pointsvertices of clipping polygon
subjected_pointsvertices of subjected polygon
result_pointsvertices of overlapping polygon
Returns
false= no overlapping polygon, true= overlapping polygon found
