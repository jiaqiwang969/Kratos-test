41  /*
42  * (simple driver)
43  * gesvd() computes the singular value decomposition (SVD) of
44  * M-by-N matrix A, optionally computing the left and/or right
45  * singular vectors. The SVD is written
46  *
47  * A = U * S * V^T or A = U * S * V^H
48  *
49  * where S is an M-by-N matrix which is zero except for its min(m,n)
50  * diagonal elements, U is an M-by-M orthogonal/unitary matrix, and V
51  * is an N-by-N orthogonal/unitary matrix. The diagonal elements of S
52  * are the singular values of A; they are real and non-negative, and
53  * are returnede in descending order. The first min(m,n) columns of
54  * U and V are the left and right singular vectors of A. (Note that
55  * the routine returns V^T or V^H, not V.
56  */
